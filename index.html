Persona

Description

Realistic representations of your target users. Should include demographic details, personal details (bio, photo), attitudinal/cognitive details (pain points, mental model), goals & motivations, and behavioral details (how they act when using the product).

Helpful links

Description

Used to organize your app or site to match the users' mental model in order to find what they're looking for. Useful for improving information architecture (e.g. navigation). Recommend 15 participants.
